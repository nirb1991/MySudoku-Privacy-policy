Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

MySudoku is designed to be a simple and enjoyable puzzle game. We are committed to protecting Your privacy.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

MySudoku is designed to collect minimal to no Personal Data from its users. We do not require account creation or collect directly identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Usage Data

When You access the App, We automatically collect certain information that helps us understand how the App is used and how to improve it. This Usage Data may include, but is not limited to:

  • Game Progress & Statistics: Information about Your gameplay, such as difficulty level played, time taken to solve puzzles, number of puzzles solved, hint usage, and mistake counts. This data is typically stored locally on Your Device.
  • Device Information: Information such as Your Device's type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data. This helps us ensure compatibility and performance across various devices.
  • Interaction Data: Information about how You interact with the App's features, such as button taps, screen views, and the duration of Your sessions.
  • Crash Data: Information about crashes and errors within the App to help us identify and fix bugs, which usually includes device information, time of crash, and crash logs.

Information Collected from Third-Party Services

MySudoku uses third-party services that collect Usage Data to help us improve the App:

  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to collect anonymized Usage Data. This data helps us understand user behavior, track app performance (e.g., number of active users, session duration, popular features), and identify areas for improvement. Firebase Analytics processes data in a way that does not directly identify any individual user. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices for Firebase at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • App Store Connect Analytics: We utilize the analytics features provided by Apple's App Store Connect to track general app usage, downloads, and sales. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

Use of Your Data

The Company uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: including to monitor the usage of our Service.
  • To improve the Application: We use analytics data (including Firebase Analytics) to understand how users engage with MySudoku. This helps us make informed decisions about feature development, optimize performance, fix bugs, and enhance the overall user experience.
  • To manage Your game progress: To save Your game progress and statistics locally on Your Device.
  • For internal analysis: To analyze usage trends and evaluate the effectiveness of new features.
  • To monitor and detect technical issues: Using crash data to identify and resolve software bugs promptly.
